About Me

Hi! My name is Ezra Bernstein, and I am a student developer interested in STEM, especially computer science, astronomy, and physics. I enjoy music, sports, and photography as well.

This site was created using UIKit, and is hosted on Google Cloud Storage. I wrote the entire site myself. All photos seen on this site were taken by me, although some are edited. Further information about its construction can be viewed on my GitHub page (link below). When I started, I had some experience with creating websites, but I was new to UIKit and Google hosting.
